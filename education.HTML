Education

Introduction

The term "education" was coined from the latin word "educo" meaning to educe,to draw out,to develop from within.Philosophers such as Socrates and authors like Napoleon Hill have described education as developing through the law of use.Knowledge is power is too trite for the more advanced and evolved world.Used knowledge ,arguably,is indeed power.

Evolution of Education

Education has changed drastically over the years.From passing on information to younger generations through word of mouth disregarging the place,apprenticeship and finally switching to formal education which takes place in learning institutions.The term "school" was coined from the Greek word "schole" meaning leisure.This gives an idea of how education was perceived back then.Perhaps as an interesting and enjoyable activity contraty to recent times where it is a neccessity.Whether it is still enjoyable or not is down to individual perception.From logograms representing whole words to letters used as components making up words.

Types of Education

Formal Education

It is the kind of learning that takes place in official settings i.e learning institutions such as universities and is often governed by set rules and regulations.Formal education back then was restricted to both gender and status i.e only the royal offsprings and sons of wealthy professionals such as physicians could access education.Currently,in most parts of the world formal education is available for both genders.

Informal Education

It is the type of learning that takes place outside the official setting and is not governed by set rules and regulations.Back then boys would accompany their fathers and through apprenticeship learn their trades.Girls on the other hand would stay back at home with their mothers where they would get educated on handling domestic chores such as housekeeping,cleaning and taking care of young children.Informal education in general has become less common over the years only to be practised mostly in the less advanced parts of the globe.

Non-formal Education

Non-formal learning includes various structured learning situations which do not either have the level of curriculum, syllabus, accreditation and certification associated with 'formal learning', but have more structure than that associated with 'informal learning', which typically take place naturally and spontaneously as part of other activities.

Importance of Education

The importance of education can not be stressed enough.It is not only beneficial at personal level but also at societal level.Along with acquiring income earning skills and satisfying self in whatever way,individuals in the society are able to develop interpersonal skills which improve the quality of interactions amongst themselves.Proper education is also a tool used to eliminate certain stereotypes,misconceptions and practices that bring more harm than good.A literate environment which is necessary for smooth daily operations is also created through education.
